Giant Virus MAGs (GVMAGs) v2

Welcome to the second release of the Giant Virus MAGs resource—a curated hub of genomes, protein families and phylogenies that shines a light on the extraordinary world of Nucleocytoviricota. Whether you’re tracking hallmark genes, benchmarking a new pipeline, or simply marveling at the viral giants’ evolutionary sprawl, everything you need starts here.

All methods, file formats and naming rules are described in our companion preprint. Feedback and citations are always appreciated! Read the preprint.
