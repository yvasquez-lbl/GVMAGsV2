Giant Virus MAGs v2

Welcome to the second release of the Giant Virus MAGs resource—a curated hub of genomes, protein families and phylogenies that shines a light on the extraordinary world of Nucleocytoviricota. Whether you’re tracking hallmark genes, benchmarking a pipeline or simply marvelling at viral giants’ evolutionary sprawl, everything you need starts here.

For full methods, file formats and naming rules, see our companion preprint. Feedback and citations are always appreciated! Read the preprint.
