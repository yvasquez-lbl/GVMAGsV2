Giant Virus MAGs v2

Welcome to the second release of the Giant Virus MAGs resource; a curated hub of genomes, protein families and phylogenies that shines a light on the extraordinary world of Nucleocytoviricota. Whether you’re tracking hallmark genes, benchmarking a pipeline or simply marvelling at giant viruses’ evolutionary sprawl, everything you need starts here.

For full methods, file formats and naming rules, see our preprint. Read the preprint.
